## Slide 2: The Problem
*   **The Gap:** Patients with vague symptoms often visit the wrong specialist or wait weeks for a GP referral.
*   **Consequence:** Delayed diagnosis, wasted money, and increased health anxiety.
*   **The Language Barrier:** In regions like India, patients often describe symptoms in "Hinglish" or local dialects which traditional search engines fail to parse correctly.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (Docvail)
*   **AI-First Discovery:** Patients describe symptoms in natural language (English, Hindi, or Hinglish).
*   **Google Gemini Integration:** We use **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash** to analyze complex symptom descriptions and instantly map them to the correct medical specialty.
*   **Direct Connection:** Once a specialty is identified, Docvail instantly displays verified doctors and hospitals in the user's city.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features (The "Wow" Factor)
1.  **Multilingual AI Analyzer:** Powered by Gemini, understanding context beyond just keywords.
2.  **Trust-Based Authentication:** Dual-Identifier Login (Email or Mobile) with OTP verification for patient security.
3.  **Modern UX:** High-fidelity Glassmorphism interface for a premium, trustworthy medical experience.
4.  **Provider Ecosystem:** Built-in support for Hospital and Doctor roles to manage their own digital presence.

---

## Slide 5: Use-Case Flow
1.  **Input:** Patient says: "Sar me bohot tez dard ho rha hai aur dhundla dikh rha h" (My head hurts a lot and I'm seeing blurry).
2.  **AI Engine:** Gemini parses the input and identifies **Ophthalmologist** or **Neurologist**.
3.  **Action:** The app automatically filters the database for these specialists in the patient's current city.
4.  **Outcome:** The patient books an appointment with a verified provider in seconds.

*   **AI/ML:** **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash API** (Natural Language Processing).
*   **Hosting:** **Firebase Hosting** (Frontend) & Render (Backend).
*   **Frontend:** React, Tailwind CSS, Vite.
*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js.
*   **Database:** MongoDB Atlas.
*   **Security:** JWT, Bcrypt, and OTP Verification.
