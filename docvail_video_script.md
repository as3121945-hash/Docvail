## Recording Tips:
1.  **Clear Audio:** Use a headset if possible. Avoid background noise.
2.  **Smooth Cursor:** Don't move your mouse too fast. Be deliberate with your clicks.
3.  **Zoom In:** If your text is small, zoom the browser to 110% or 125% so it's readable in the video.
4.  **No Dead Air:** If a page takes a second to load (Render cold start!), edit that pause out or keep talking through it.
